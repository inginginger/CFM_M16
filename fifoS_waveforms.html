Sample behavioral waveforms for design file fifoS.v

The following waveforms show the behavior of scfifo megafunction for the chosen set of parameters in design fifoS.v. The design fifoS.v has a depth of 2 words of 8 bits each. The fifo is in show-ahead synchronous mode. The data becomes available before 'rdreq' is asserted; 'rdreq' acts as a read acknowledge.
